Blue Mountain Brewery Sour Banchee
Pale Sour Ale
ABV: 7.0%, IBUs: 30, OG: 16.5

Sour Banchee melds a sharply sour nose with a surprisingly balanced flavor of citrusy hops, light and medium crystal malts and a residual soft taste of bourbon and oak. The base beer is hopped with a heavy hand of European and Pacific Northwest whole flower hops, with an addition of Southern Hemisphere hops added during the barrel aging. Then the beer is soured in the barrel for over 9 months.
Blue Mountain Brewery Blue Reserve
Hommelbier / Belgian Pale Ale
ABV: 7.0%, IBUs: 44, OG: 16.4

Belgian yeast, pale malts, mountain water and American Cascade hops make for a simple beer with extraordinary flavor, worthy of our Reserve title each year. Spicy yeast and citrusy hops in a bright golden beer.

Elysian Brewing Company Makana Imperial Porter (First time ever outside brewery!)
Imperial Porter
ABV: 8.0%, IBUs: 40

The honeymoon is over...
The idea for this beer was a parting gift from the Hawaiian islands, where craft breweries large and small all brew some rendition of a dark beer with native coconut. This imperial porter is backed by C45, Black, kiln coffee, & chocolate rye malts, bittered with Simcoe & GNB to a subtle balance, and finished with over a pound of hand-toasted coconut per barrel. Post fermentation, it was conditioned on vanilla beans, adding another layer of ono. This beer will surely ease the pain of reality when back on the mainland.
