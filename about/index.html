About Raleigh Rare & Vintage Beer Tasting

The Raleigh Rare & Vintage Beer Tasting was created to provide the beer enthusiast with the ultimate beer experience! We are providing 40 Rare and/or Vintage beers poured by brewery representatives in an intimate environment, allowing attendants the opportunity to learn what makes these beers special and those that brewed them.

Appetizers, bottled water and sodas are included in the ticket price. Attendance is limited and tickets will sell fast. A link to purchase tickets will be available on November 1 on this site.

A list of beers being brought by participating breweries can be found on the Breweries & Beers page.


A percentage of the net proceeds from ticket sales will be going towards the awareness mission of Pint for Prostates, a 501(c)3 non-profit charity founded by beer writer and prostate cancer survivor Rick Lyke. Last year we raised almost $6K and hope to raise much more this year!

Do I have to be 21 or older to attend?

Yes, without exceptions! All IDs will be checked at the door.

Can I bring pets?

Please leave your pets at home as they will not be permitted.

What happens if it rains?

The event will be under a tent with heaters and will take place rain or shine!

Will there be food at the event?

Yes there will be a food available. Additionally, Tyler's and other restaurants (18 Seaboard, Peace China, J.Betskis, Kimbop, Bad Daddy's) are within walking distance.

Where should I stay if I need accommodation?

We are happy to be partnering once again with the Sheraton Raleigh hotel.

How should I get around?

Experience the R-LINE, an eco-friendly approach to urban transportation. This FREE circulator service features hybrid electric buses that will connect you to The Raleigh Rare & Vintage Beer Tasting (Seaboard Station – R3) from the Sheraton (Raleigh Convention Center – R13).
